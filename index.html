About Us

A newly established company located in Guwahati - the heart of Assam and the gateway to the North East of India. Zayne is started by a highly qualified and experienced professional from the service and payment industry with a goal to simplify and ease property ownership and management thereby helping home owners living away to maximize returns through safe and secure processes.

What we offer ?

Tenant management

Tenant search, facilitate tenant move-in/out, police verification, legal documentations and society formalities, seamless renewal agreement or tenant replacement

Property management

Inventory checks both interior & exterior at the time of tenant move-in and exit, minor and regular repair/maintenance, six monthly inspection visits and upkeep of the property, Providing real time video and photos to owners. Vendor management

Rent & Deposit management

Collection of security deposit & monthly rent. Rent transfer, bill payment and society payment. Exit settlement
